Our restaurants do not only sell food; they showcase, educate and teach their culture through food. With only one (1) restaurant per country ~ we are carefully selecting the best of the best for our attendees!
Around The World Cultural Food Festival has food (including dessert) vending space for about 20 vendors who provide a variety of ethnic foods to our patrons during the festival. Many of our regular vendors return each year so anyone interested in becoming a food vendor must apply. If we get an opening, we will fill it according to our need to maintain a variety of options for the festival food booths. The deadline for booths to submit their application and pay the fees is December 31, 2018.  Contact info@aroundtheworldfestival or fill out the form online to inquire or get on the waiting list.

Food Vendors are selected by a selection committee. Please apply to be considered for the festival. If you were selected in 2019-2020 and are in good standing with ATW, you are pre-approved for 2021.

If you have not already been selected, you may apply and will be subject to the selection process depending on available slots and we will also take into consideration your application’s timeliness and whether your particular menu matches well with the diversity of the ATW food offerings.

Please note: Registration is NOT completed and you are NOT considered for the event until the Vendor Registration Fee has been paid. If you are not accepted your booth fee will be refunded.
